🔍 Short Profiles (Explanations)

1) 🩸 Hypovolemic Shock

CausesBlood loss, dehydration, burns, vomiting/diarrhea
Preload (CVP / PAWP)⬇️⬇️
Afterload (SVR)⬆️⬆️ (compensatory vasospasm)
CO / CI⬇️⬇️
LVEF↔️ / ⬆️ (hyperdynamic due to low volume)
Key SignsCollapsed veins, thirst, dry mucous membranes
Fluid Responsiveness✅ Positive

2) ❤️ Cardiogenic Shock

CausesMI, cardiomyopathy, severe arrhythmias, myocarditis
Preload⬆️⬆️ (congestion)
Afterload (SVR)⬆️⬆️
CO / CI⬇️⬇️ (CI < 1.8)
LVEF⬇️⬇️ (<40%, often <25%)
Key SignsWet rales, pulmonary edema, engorged neck veins
Fluid Responsiveness❌ Negative
